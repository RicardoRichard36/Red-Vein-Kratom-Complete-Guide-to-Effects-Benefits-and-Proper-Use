## What Is Red Vein Kratom? üåø

Red vein kratom represents the most mature leaves harvested from the kratom tree, scientifically known as Mitragyna speciosa. These leaves develop their characteristic red veining as they reach full maturity, typically remaining on the tree longer than leaves used for green or white vein varieties.
